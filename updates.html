Updates¶

If you’d like to stay up to date on the community and development of Eve, there are several options:

GitHub¶

The best way to track the development of Eve is through the GitHub repo.

Twitter¶

I often tweet about new features and releases of Eve. Follow @nicolaiarocci for updates.

IRC¶

The official Freenode channel for Eve is #evehq

You are looking at the documentation of the development version.
